Travel Consent Forms Notarization Services

When a minor travels internationally without both parents, many countries require a notarized letter of consent from the absent parent(s). Our fast and professional notarization service ensures your travel consent forms are properly authorized for international travel.

Who Needs Travel Consent Forms?

  • Minors traveling with one parent and not the other
  • Minors traveling with grandparents or other relatives
  • Minors traveling with non-parental guardians
  • Young adults (18-21) traveling to certain countries
  • Families traveling internationally to countries requiring consent documentation

What Information Goes in a Travel Consent Letter?

  • Child's full name and date of birth
  • Passport number and expiration date
  • Destination country and travel dates
  • Name of person(s) traveling with the child
  • Parental signature and notarization
  • Contact information of absent parent(s)

Our Travel Consent Notarization Process

  1. Prepare Letter: Draft your travel consent letter with all required information
  2. Verify Identity: We confirm the parent's identity with government-issued ID
  3. Witness Signature: Parent signs the letter in our presence
  4. Apply Notary Seal: We notarize with our official seal and signature
  5. Provide Copies: You receive certified copies for your trip

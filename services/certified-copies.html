Certified True Copies Notarization Services

A certified true copy is an official copy of an original document that has been certified by a notary public as a true and accurate duplicate. Many institutions require certified true copies of important documents for verification and security purposes.

What Can Be Certified as a True Copy?

  • Identity Documents: Passports, driver's licenses, birth certificates, marriage certificates
  • Financial Documents: Bank statements, tax documents, proof of income letters
  • Educational Documents: Diplomas, degrees, transcripts, certificates
  • Contracts & Agreements: Lease agreements, employment contracts, purchase agreements
  • Medical Records: Doctor letters, vaccination records, health certifications
  • Professional Licenses: Professional certifications and license copies
  • Corporate Documents: Articles of incorporation, bylaws, board resolutions

Common Uses for Certified True Copies

  • Immigration and visa applications
  • Professional license applications
  • Financial and banking transactions
  • Employment verification
  • Real estate and property transactions
  • Court proceedings and legal matters
  • Educational enrollment

Our Certified Copy Process

Remote Online Notarization (RON) Services

Remote Online Notarization (RON) allows you to get documents notarized digitally from anywhere in Ontario. Using secure video conferencing, we verify your identity and witness your digital signature, providing a fast and convenient notarization solution.

What is Remote Online Notarization?

Remote Online Notarization is a legally valid process where a notary public verifies your identity and witnesses your digital signature through a secure video conference. The entire process happens online, making notarization accessible from home or office without the need to travel or meet in person.

Documents You Can Notarize Online

  • Affidavits and statutory declarations
  • Power of attorney documents
  • Consents and permissions
  • Loan documents and financial agreements
  • Real estate documents (many types)
  • Business agreements
  • Signature verifications
  • Various other legal documents

How Remote Online Notarization Works

  1. Schedule Appointment: Book your RON appointment online at your convenient time
  2. Prepare Documents: Have your unsigned documents ready in digital format
  3. Video Conference: Join a secure video call with our notary at your scheduled time
  4. Identity Verification: Notary verifies your identity using government-issued ID
  5. Document Review: Discuss the document and confirm it's ready for notarization
  6. Digital Signature: You digitally sign the document during the video call
  7. Notarization: Notary applies digital signature and seal
  8. Receive Documents: Notarized documents are sent to you electronically

Advantages of RON Services

  • Convenience: Notarize documents from anywhere—home, office, or anywhere in Ontario
  • Speed: Same-day appointments often available
  • No Travel: Eliminate travel time and expenses
  • Flexibility: Choose appointment times that work for you, including evenings
  • Security: Encrypted video and digital signatures ensure document security
  • Fast Delivery: Receive notarized documents electronically within hours
  • Legally Valid: RON documents have full legal standing in Ontario

Requirements for RON

  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Internet connection and device with camera/microphone
  • Digital version of documents to be notarized
  • Private location for the video conference
  • Willingness to sign digitally (e-signature)

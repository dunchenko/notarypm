Our Business Notarization Process

  1. Verify Authorization: Confirm signatory authority and business legitimacy
  2. Review Documents: Examine all business documents for completeness and accuracy
  3. Verify Identity: Confirm identity of signing corporate representatives
  4. Witness Signatures: Signatories sign documents in our presence
  5. Apply Notary Seal: Official notarization with seal and signature
  6. Provide Certified Copies: You receive official certified copies
